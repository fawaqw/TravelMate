

## App Name
**TravelMate**

## Short Description
Discover your next adventure. Explore cities, read reviews, and save your favorite travel spots.

## Full Description
TravelMate is your personal guide to the world's most exciting destinations. Whether you're planning a weekend getaway or a world tour, TravelMate helps you discover hidden gems and popular landmarks.

**Key Features:**
*   **Explore:** Browse a curated feed of beautiful locations worldwide.
*   **Search:** Find cities across the globe with our fast and intuitive search engine.
*   **Real-time Reviews:** Share your experiences and read feedback from other travelers.
*   **Favorites:** Save places you love to your personal favorites list for quick access.
*   **Offline Mode:** Access your cached data even when you're off the grid.

## Key Features
1. **Home Screen:** A vibrant list of destinations with high-quality images.
2. **Search Screen:** Real-time city search results.
3. **Details Screen:** In-depth info about a place with user reviews.
4. **Favorites:** A collection of the user's saved spots.

## Category
Travel & Local

## Privacy Policy Notes
*   We use Firebase Authentication to secure your account.
*   User data (favorites/reviews) is stored securely in Firebase Realtime Database.
*   No personal data is shared with third parties.
*   Location data is only used to enhance search results.
